The Career Crisis Nobody Talks About

Close your eyes and think back to Class 10. Someone asked you: "What do you want to become?" Your options felt limited to: Doctor. Engineer. CA. Lawyer. Teacher. Maybe your parents pushed you toward one. Maybe your friends influenced you. Maybe you just picked what felt "safe."

But here's the truth nobody told you:

There are over 500 different career paths in India. Careers that don't require you to crack JEE or NEET. Careers where you can earn well, work remotely, travel the world, or solve problems that excite you. Careers like Ethical Hacker. Food Stylist. Wildlife Photographer. Sign Language Interpreter. You never chose from 500 options. You chose from 5. That's the problem we're solving.

The Real Numbers

These aren't just numbers. These are real students making life-changing decisions based on incomplete information. We believe every student deserves to KNOW what's possible before they decide.

What is Career Kuch Hatke?

Career Kuch Hatke is India's first student-built platform dedicated to unconventional career discovery. We don't sell courses. We don't promise jobs. We don't spam you with ads. What we DO:

  • ✅Showcase 25 handpicked careers across 5 categories (Creative, Tech, Science, Business, Unique)
  • ✅Explain EACH career in simple language — what they do, how to get in, salary ranges, and real people doing it
  • ✅Provide a fun 2-minute quiz that suggests careers based on YOUR personality
  • ✅Share FREE resources — YouTube channels, courses, books — to help you start exploring
  • ✅Make career discovery EXCITING, not stressful

We're not here to tell you WHAT to choose. We're here to show you WHAT EXISTS. The choice? That's always yours.

Why I Built This

Hi, I'm Sneha Mahendra Prajapati. I'm a First Year BSc Computer Science student. But honestly? I almost wasn't.

After Class 10, I scored 88%. Everyone congratulated me. But here's what they didn't see: I studied through COVID lockdowns. Concepts in Physics and Chemistry flew over my head. I had zero clarity about Science. Still, I chose Computer Science after 10th. Not because I loved it. Because "everyone said Science has scope." Result? 67% in Class 12. Complete confusion about what to do next.

After 12th, I discovered Hotel Management. I was genuinely interested. Finally, something clicked. But the fees were high. My family couldn't afford it. My parents asked: "Then why did you take CS after 10th? Why did we spend so much?" I had no answer. So I took BSc Computer Science. Not out of passion. Out of pressure and guilt.

Today, I don't blame my parents. They did their best. I blame the system that forced me to choose at 15 without showing me what else existed. When I got this college project, I knew what I wanted to build: the website I wish existed when I was confused in Class 10. Career Kuch Hatke is my way of saying: "Explore before you decide. Your interests matter. It's okay to choose something hatke." I can't change my past. But maybe I can help you avoid my mistakes. If even one student explores careers because of this site, this project was worth it.

— Sneha Mahendra Prajapati FYBSc Computer Science [April, 2025]
